|---|---|---|---|---|---|---|---|---|---|---|---|---|---|---|---|
| 1 | 2 | 4 | 2 | 3 | 1 | 5 | 2 | 6 | 8 | 3 | 5 | 4 | 1 | 2 | 6 |

- First In First Out (FIFO)
- Clock Algorithm
